Sweet Smoked Pork Ribs

Description

This smoked rib recipe uses a sweet and spicy dry rub to bring out all the flavors. Slow cooked for 4 hours to have the meat falling off the bone.

Ingredients

Pork Ribs

  • 1/4 cup salt
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 2 tbsp packed brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp ground white pepper
  • 2 tbsp onion powder
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tbsp ground paprika
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 10 lbs baby back pork ribs

Sauce

  • 1 cup apple juice
  • 1/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup bbq sauce

Steps

  1. 1.Gather all ingredients
  2. 2.Prepare ribs: Stir all spices together in a small bowl
  3. 3.Rub spice mixture all over ribs; then cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 mins
  4. 4.Make Sauce: Stir apple juice, brown sugar, bbq sauce together until well combined
  5. 5.Unwrap ribs and arrange the ribs on racks in smoker. Fill smoker pan with any flavor smoking chips and heat to 270 degrees F. Cook ribs for 1 hour.
  6. 6.Brush sauce on ribs. Continue cooking ribs and brushing sauce every 30 to 40 minutes, until meat is no longer pink and begins to shrink back from the bone, 3 to 4 hours.
  7. 7.Brush ribs one last time and remove from smoker. Wrap ribs in aluminum foil for 10 to 15 minutes ti allow juices to reabsorb into the meat.
  8. Enjoy!
