Ground Beef Tacos

Description

Delicious Ground Beef Tacos that are super quick and easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 pack of seasoning (your choice)
  • 3/4 cup of water
  • Taco shells
  • 1 diced onion
  • Shredded Lettuce
  • Shredded Cheese

Steps

  1. Brown the meat by breaking up the beef in a pan
  2. Once meat is all brown add water and taco seasoning
  3. Mix until mix thickens and there's very little liquid at the bottom of the pan
  4. Construct the taco and enjoy!
